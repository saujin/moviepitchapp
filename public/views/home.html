Start Pitching

What is a pitch?

A pitch is a verbal explanation of a movie you hope to sell to a studio.

What makes a great pitch?

A great pitch doesn't have to be long, sometimes a sentence or two is all it takes. Aim for an intriguing idea that can be easily stated in a few words and is easily understood by all.

Examples of Great Pitches

  • A woman and her sled-dogs race 1,000 miles through the wilderness to deliver medicine to a rural Alaskan town.
  • A talented golden retriever makes the middle-school basketball team.
  • A man discovers his whole life is a set for a reality TV show.
