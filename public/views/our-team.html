Meet Our Producers

BOB KOSBERG

Chairman and Founder of Moviepitch.com

Bob is responsible for creative affairs. Bob has broad experience as a film and TV writer and producer. He has sold over 90 film and TV projects during his career with a very active current slate. He has worked as a creative executive and producer at Warner Brothers/Guber-Peters, Paramount, Sony Pictures, Tri-Star, Merv Griffin Entertainment, Constantin Film and Nash Entertainment. He continues to do speaking events all over the world on screenwriting and pitching/selling ideas to Hollywood.

Jonas Goodman

CEO

Jonas is responsible for managing all business and creative affairs. Jonas has broad experience as a film, television and new media executive and producer; working for directors (including Alan Pakula, Michael Radford) and production companies inside the studio system and independently (including Front Street Pictures, Miramax, Atlas Entertainment) and building effective collaborations between the film and tech communities.
